In 20 Years, the King of Malaysia Might be a Libertarian Pianist.

On January 6th, 2019, 'He Who is Made Lord'[] unmade himself for the first time ever. In a historic first, Yang di-Pertuan Agong Muhammad V, King and head-of-state of Malaysia, abdicated the throne following rumors about a secret marriage to a former Miss Moscow beauty pageant winner. Dubbed "The People's Sultan[] for his down-to-earth mannerisms--Muhammad was well-known for sponsoring charity walkathons and his preference for traditional Malay dress over the usual regal garb--and his unusual hobbies like offroading and archery, the King's resignation came as a shock to many.
